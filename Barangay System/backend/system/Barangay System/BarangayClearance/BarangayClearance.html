A Cedula, or Community Tax Certificate (CTC), is a legal identification document in the Philippines issued to individuals or corporations upon payment of a community tax. It is required for many government transactions.

Application for Cedula

FAQs

  • Who can get it: Any individual 18+ or corporation.
  • Where to get it: City/Municipal Treasurer's Office.
  • Requirements: Valid ID and application form.
  • Cost: Based on income.
  • Validity: Valid until end of the year.
