ABOUT SINAG SPORTS

SINAG SPORTS 2025 is our school’s annual sports festival celebrating teamwork, passion, and excellence in athletics. This year’s event brings together students, teachers, and staff in a vibrant showcase of talent, sportsmanship, and unity. Through friendly competition and spirited games, SINAG SPORTS 2025 aims to promote a healthy lifestyle, strengthen camaraderie, and ignite the flame of school pride in every participant. (GAWA NG AI)
